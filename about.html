I chose to link the "mapping police violence" site as a system of domination because of how police violence has been seen as a means of subjugation. The site tracks insteances of police violence and death with state by state statistics and a cache of related data. I chose to link the site to the Jamaican Slave revolt in the 18th century as a menas of liberation, because it represetned the overthrowing of opressive forces in pursuit of liberation. The site details the counterinsurgency and maps what led to the rebellion and the subsequent fallout thet led to the deaths of five hundred Black people in battle. Rethinking design education begins with unlearning colonialism in new media, and the students at UCLA have utilized digital ethnography to examine how social movements have utilized social media to promote messages of justice. The final link details the 'Race and the Digital' project which serves to educate viewers of ethnicity and inclusion in the humanities.
